What Is a
Bloom Bar?

A bloom bar is a beautifully styled floral experience that invites guests to create their own bouquets from a curated selection of fresh seasonal blooms.

It’s part activity, part décor, and part take-home favor, designed to make your event feel both elevated and memorable.

Vermont roots, floral heart.
Our Story

The Heart Behind
Petal & Stem

Born and raised in Vermont, I grew up surrounded by nature, so flowers have always felt like home to me.

During COVID, while home with my two young children, I found myself craving a creative outlet, something that brought beauty and calm into the everyday. Floral arranging quickly became that for me.

As I began sharing my arrangements with friends and family, their encouragement helped me realize this could be something more.

Petal & Stem was created from a love of gathering, beauty, and thoughtfully designed experiences. My goal is to bring a bloom bar experience that feels romantic, elevated, and memorable for every celebration.
